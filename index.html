Hello! My name is Deemanuel Faustin but you can just call me Dee! Ever since I finished playing Sonic Rivals on the PSP I have had a great interest in game development. Not only the back-end aspect of creating the video games I grew up with but also the artistic work that goes into bringing those games to life. From there I would be hard at work mocking up ideas for my own video games in hopes of pursuing a career in game development.

Countless notebooks and sketchbooks later that dream still held strong. I have graduated from The Tech Academy Game Development Bootcamp leaving the program with new and bolstered knowledge in both the Unity and Unreal Engine. To this day I still chase the dream of being part of a team that is responsible for creating something that insprires someone just like the games of my childhood.

  • C#
  • C++
  • HTML
